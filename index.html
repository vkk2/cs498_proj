In professional sports, a salary cap is an agreement or rule that places a limit on the amount of money that a team can spend on players salaries. It exists as a per player limit or a total limit for the team's roster, or both. Salary caps can be a big issue in negotiations between league management and players unions because they limit players and teams ability to negotiate higher salaries even if a team is operating at significant profits.




This project attempts to study this salary comparison between the teams, and observing if they contribute to higher wins or not. If they do, it drills down on how player rating influences these
